Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I feed my koi?

Feed your koi 2-4 times a day during warm months, and reduce feeding in colder months.

What's the ideal water temperature for koi?

Koi thrive in water temperatures between 59-77°F (15-25°C).

How can I prevent algae growth in my koi pond?

Maintain proper filtration, avoid overfeeding, and consider adding aquatic plants to compete with algae for nutrients.
