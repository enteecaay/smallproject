Seasonal Koi Care Guide

Spring Care

As temperatures rise, gradually increase feeding. Clean the pond and check filtration systems. Monitor water quality closely as the ecosystem rebalances.

Summer Care

Ensure proper aeration and shade to keep water temperatures stable. Feed koi 2-4 times daily. Regularly check for and remove any algae growth.

Fall Care

Reduce feeding as temperatures drop. Remove fallen leaves from the pond. Prepare the pond for winter by cleaning and checking equipment.

Winter Care

In colder climates, use a pond heater or de-icer to maintain an opening in the ice. Stop feeding when water temperatures drop below 50°F (10°C). Ensure the pond has adequate depth for fish to overwinter.
